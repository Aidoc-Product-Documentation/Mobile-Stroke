Creating Patient Cards in Mobile Stroke Feed

Patient Cards in the Stroke Mobile Feed provide a one-glance view of Patient details and indicate if imaging and reports are available for the patient. Patient Cards also display which AI flags were flagged for the patient by Aidoc AI algorithms
