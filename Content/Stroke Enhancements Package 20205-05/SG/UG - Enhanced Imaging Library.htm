Enhanced Imaging Library

The Imaging Library feature provides Aidoc users with a more comprehensive view of individual patient data, rendering relevant information more accessible to clinicians as they make patient care decisions.

Imaging Availability in the Mobile App

Imaging Series analyzed by Aidoc AI Algorithms - All imaging series analyzed by Aidoc algorithms since launch of mobile flow at facility are available in the Imaging Library

Imaging Series NOT analyzed by Aidoc AI Algorithms - Imaging series that have not been analyzed by Aidoc algorithms from current date/time and 7 days back are available in the Imaging Library

Accessing the Imaging Library

To access the Imaging Library, click on a Patient Card in the mobile feed to open the Image Viewer, then click on the Imaging Library icon to open the library dialog. Click on any series to open it in the Image Viewer.
