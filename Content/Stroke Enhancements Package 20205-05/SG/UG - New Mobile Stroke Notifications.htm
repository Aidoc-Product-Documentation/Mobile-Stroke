New Mobile Stroke Notifications

The Mobile Stroke App offers three new notifications that allow clinical teams to focus on relevant cases, and supports clinicians in managing emergent situations effectively.

Notification Description Default New/Legacy
First Suspected Stroke Imaging First stroke-related imaging series added to Patient Card On/Regular New
ICH Head NCCT with suspected ICH AI flag available in the app. Off New
BA Head CTA with suspected BA AI flag available in the app. Off New
VO Head CTA with suspected VO AI flag available in the app. On/Siren Legacy
CTP CTP imaging is available in the app. On/Regular Legacy

Editing Mobile Stroke App Notification Settings

You can change the default setting (ON/OFF) and sound (Regular/Siren) of the mobile stroke notifications via the Preferences menu.

To edit the mobile app notification settings, click on the Home Screen Preferences menu, then click on Stroke Flow Notifications dialog. Modify notification settings/sound.
